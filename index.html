Hello World!

I am passionate about neurodegenerative diseases. I am a skilled computational neuroscientist and hold a PhD in Parkinson's disease. I analyse data of large longitudinal study cohorts with respect to clinical, genetic, and brain imaging data.
Currently, I am working as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the NIH and am part of the collaborative efforts at the Laboraty of Neurogenetics (LNG) and the Center for Alzheimer's and Related Dementias (CARD).
I am a quick learner and a team worker that gets the job done.

My main interests are biomarkers for disease progression and patient stratification from a clinical, genetic, and brain imaging point of view to further the improvement of personalised treatments.
